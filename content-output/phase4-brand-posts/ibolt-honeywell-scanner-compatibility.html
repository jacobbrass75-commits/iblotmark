iBOLT Confirms Barcode Scanner Mounting Solutions Compatible with Honeywell, Zebra, and Symbol Scanners

Published February 5, 2026

iBOLT has completed compatibility testing for its XL Barcode Scanner Holder line across the most widely deployed handheld barcode scanners in warehouse and retail environments. The XL Scanner holders are confirmed to securely fit scanners from Honeywell, Zebra, and Symbol (now part of Zebra Technologies), including the specific models most commonly found on forklifts, packing stations, and receiving docks.

Confirmed Compatible Scanners

iBOLT's XL Barcode Scanner Holders use an adjustable cradle designed to accommodate the body dimensions of full-size handheld scanners. The following models have been tested and confirmed compatible:

Honeywell

  • Honeywell Voyager 1200g / 1202g
  • Honeywell Voyager 1400g / 1450g / 1472g
  • Honeywell Hyperion 1300g
  • Honeywell Xenon 1900 / 1950
  • Honeywell Granit 1280i / 1911i

Zebra

  • Zebra DS2208
  • Zebra DS4308
  • Zebra DS8108 / DS8178
  • Zebra LI2208
  • Zebra LS2208 (legacy, still widely deployed)

Symbol (Zebra Technologies)

  • Symbol LS2208
  • Symbol DS6708
  • Symbol LS4208

The XL holder's adjustable cradle grip accommodates scanner bodies ranging from approximately 60mm to 105mm in width, covering the vast majority of standard handheld scanners currently in commercial use.

Four Mounting Configurations for Every Workspace

iBOLT offers the XL Barcode Scanner Holder in four distinct base configurations, so warehouse operations can choose the mounting method that best fits their equipment and workflow.

XL Forklift Scanner Holder (38mm Mount)

The iBOLT XL Forklift Barcode Scanner Holder ($79.95) is designed for permanent installation on forklift overhead guards, masts, and cabs. The 38mm (C-size) ball connection provides the stability and vibration resistance needed for active forklift use. Operators can grab the scanner, scan a pallet, and return it to the holder without looking, because the holder keeps the scanner in the same position every time.

iBOLT XL Forklift Barcode Scanner Holder - $79.95

XL Scanner triMag (Magnetic Mount)

The iBOLT XL Scanner triMag ($67.95) uses strong dual magnets in a low-profile design that attaches to any ferrous metal surface. This is ideal for packing stations, receiving desks, and steel shelving where a permanent bolt-on installation is not practical. The magnetic base holds the scanner securely but allows repositioning in seconds.

iBOLT XL Scanner triMag Magnetic Mount - $67.95

XL Scanner BizMount (Heavy-Duty Magnetic)

The iBOLT XL Scanner BizMount ($80.00) steps up to an 88mm heavy-duty magnetic base for environments where standard magnets may not provide enough hold. The BizMount's larger magnetic footprint delivers increased holding force on forklifts, steel workbenches, and heavy equipment. It is particularly well suited for warehouse vehicles that traverse rough concrete floors or dock plates.

iBOLT XL Scanner BizMount Magnetic Mount - $80.00

XL Scanner IncrediBOLT 360 (Magnetic with Arm)

The iBOLT XL Scanner IncrediBOLT 360 ($83.95) combines the 88mm magnetic base with iBOLT's signature IncrediBOLT 360 arm, providing full 360-degree rotation and multi-angle positioning. This configuration is ideal for operators who need to adjust their scanner's reach and angle depending on the task, such as scanning items at varying heights on a pallet rack.

Dual-Device Mounting: Tablet Plus Scanner

One of iBOLT's key advantages in the warehouse space is the ability to mount a tablet and a barcode scanner side by side on the same piece of equipment. Using iBOLT's 38mm dual-arm adapters, a forklift operator can have their inventory management tablet mounted on one arm and their Honeywell or Zebra scanner holder on the other, both connected to the same base plate. This keeps both devices within arm's reach and eliminates the need for separate mounting hardware from different manufacturers.

Because iBOLT uses industry-standard ball sizes across its entire product line, the same 38mm C-size base that holds a scanner holder today can accept a TabDock tablet holder, a phone mount, or a custom bracket from iBOLT's catalog of over 300 modular parts.
