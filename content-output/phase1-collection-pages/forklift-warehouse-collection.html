Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best forklift tablet mount for warehouses?

The iBOLT Dock'n Lock Bizmount is the most popular choice for warehouse forklifts. It clamps directly to forklift pillars, locks the tablet in place for security, and uses heavy-gauge steel construction that handles daily vibration without loosening. For operators who prefer a seat-level mount, the LockPro FlexPro attaches to seat rails instead.

What makes iBOLT forklift mounts different from RAM Mounts?

iBOLT forklift mounts are purpose-built for warehouse and material handling environments with industrial-grade steel and aluminum construction. The modular 38mm ball system is cross-compatible with industry-standard components, and iBOLT offers dedicated accessories like barcode scanner holders that integrate directly into the same mounting system. Every mount includes security locking as a standard feature.

How do you install an iBOLT forklift pillar mount?

Most installations take under 15 minutes. The Bizmount uses heavy-duty clamps that wrap around forklift pillars or roll cage tubes. You position the clamp, tighten the bolts with a standard wrench, attach the ball arm, and snap in the tablet holder. No drilling or welding required.

Can iBOLT forklift mounts handle daily warehouse vibration?

Yes. Every iBOLT forklift mount is designed and tested for the sustained vibration of material handling equipment. The ball-and-socket joints use tension-locking mechanisms that maintain their position through bumps and vibration, and the steel mounting plates distribute force across a wide contact area to prevent stress fractures.

What's the difference between the Bizmount, LockPro, and IncrediBOLT forklift mounts?

The Bizmount line uses heavy-gauge steel with powder coating and clamps to forklift pillars. The LockPro line features aluminum construction with a premium locking mechanism and mounts to seat rails or other attachment points. The IncrediBOLT line adds 360-degree micro-adjustability for operators who need precise angle control. All three lines share the same modular component system.

Do iBOLT forklift mounts work with iPads and Samsung tablets?

Yes. The universal tablet holders fit any tablet from 7 to 10 inches, including all iPad models (iPad, iPad Air, iPad Pro 11"), Samsung Galaxy Tab A and S series, and ruggedized tablets from Zebra, Honeywell, and Samsung. Device-specific holders are also available for tighter fits.

Can I add a barcode scanner holder to my forklift mount?

Yes. The iBOLT XL Barcode Scanner Holder uses the same 38mm ball system, so it mounts directly alongside your existing tablet mount on the same forklift pillar. This keeps both your tablet and scanner within arm's reach without adding a separate mounting system.
