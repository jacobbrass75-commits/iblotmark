How do I mount a barcode scanner on a warehouse vehicle?

You have two main options. For forklift pillars and roll cages, use the XL Forklift Scanner Holder with a 38mm pillar clamp. What's the difference between the standard and XL scanner holder?

The XL scanner holder has a wider, deeper cradle designed to fit the bulkier barcode scanners commonly used in warehouses and distribution centers. It accommodates pistol-grip scanners and models with larger battery packs that won't fit in a standard-size holster.

Do iBOLT scanner mounts work with wireless barcode scanners?

Yes. The holster is a physical cradle that holds the scanner body regardless of whether it connects via Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, or USB. Wireless scanners sit in the holster between scans, and the open design allows easy one-handed grab-and-return without disconnecting anything.
