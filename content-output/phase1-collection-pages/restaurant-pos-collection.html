What is the best tablet mount for restaurants?

The iBOLT Tablet Tower system is purpose-built for restaurant environments. It holds multiple tablets vertically in a single station, keeping your counter organized while giving staff quick access to every delivery app and POS terminal. The locking holders keep devices secure, and the modular design lets you add or remove tablets as your needs change.

How do I organize multiple food delivery app tablets?

A vertical Tablet Tower is the most efficient approach. Rather than scattering individual tablets across your counter, the iBOLT Quad Tablet Tower holds up to four devices in one compact footprint. Each tablet sits at a readable angle, and the whole system takes up less space than a single cash register.

What is the iBOLT Tablet Tower and how does it work?

The Tablet Tower is a modular, vertical mounting system. You select a base (weighted, C-clamp, or drill mount), attach a vertical pole, and stack tablet holders at the heights you want. Each holder adjusts to fit tablets from 7 to 10 inches and locks in place for security. The entire assembly takes about 10 minutes with no special tools.

Do iBOLT restaurant mounts work with Square, Toast, and Shopify POS?

Yes. iBOLT tablet holders use a universal cradle design that fits any 7 to 10 inch tablet, so they work with Square, Toast, Shopify POS, Lightspeed, Clover, and any other POS app running on a standard tablet.

How many tablets can an iBOLT Tablet Tower hold?

The standard Tablet Tower holds up to four tablets. Wall-mounted versions are available in three-holder and four-holder configurations. Because the system is modular, you can start with fewer holders and add more as needed.

What's the best way to mount tablets in a food truck?

For food trucks, the drill-base Tablet Tower or the LockPro Drill Base POS Stand provide the most stability. Suction and weighted bases can shift during transit, but a drill-mounted solution stays locked in place whether you're driving between locations or serving a lunch rush.

Can I customize my iBOLT Tablet Tower for more or fewer tablets?

Absolutely. The Tablet Tower is fully modular. Every component (bases, poles, and tablet holders) is sold separately, so you can build a two-tablet station today and expand to four tomorrow without replacing anything you already own.
