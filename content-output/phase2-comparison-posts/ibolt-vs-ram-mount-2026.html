The "Specialist vs Generalist" Framework

The clearest way to think about this comparison: RAM Mount is a generalist. They make mounts for everything. iBOLT is a specialist. They make mounts for business and industrial applications.

Generalists offer breadth. You can mount anything, anywhere, with RAM. Specialists offer depth. iBOLT's forklift mounts, Tablet Towers, scanner holders, and POS stations are products that a generalist would never build because the market for each one is too specific. But if you are in that specific market (and you are reading this, so you probably are), the purpose-built solution will serve you better.

This is not a knock on RAM. It is a recognition that different companies solve different problems. A general-purpose hospital is not "worse" than a cardiac surgery center. They serve different needs.
